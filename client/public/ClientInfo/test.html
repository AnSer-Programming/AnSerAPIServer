How to Answer Your Calls

Standard Account Greeting: "Thank you for calling [Business Name]. Our office is currently closed. We are here from [Day & Time]. If you have an administrative or non-urgent matter, please call back during our normal operation hours. For all urgent matters, please press 5 to speak with our answering service. Goodbye."

Standard Auto Answer: "Thank you for calling [Business Name]. All of our phone agents are currently busy assisting other callers. Please hold, and we will be with you shortly. We sincerely appreciate your patience as we handle each caller in the order received."
